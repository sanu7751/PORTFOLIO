Student Biodata System

The Student Biodata System allows students to manage and update their personal information and academic details. It provides a user-friendly interface to add biodata, including name, contact information, education details, and other relevant information.

How the System Works:

The system enables students to input their biodata through an intuitive interface. It stores the information securely in a relational database, making it easily accessible and editable for students and authorized personnel.

Steps in the Biodata Process:

  • Student Registration: Students can register and create their profiles.
  • Information Entry: Students input their personal and academic details.
  • Data Management: Administrators can manage and update student biodata when needed.
  • Data Retrieval: Students and administrators can retrieve and edit stored biodata.

Key Features:

  • Easy-to-use interface for students to update and manage their biodata.
  • Secure data storage for sensitive student information.
  • Admin panel for managing student records and updates.
